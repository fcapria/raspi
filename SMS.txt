ompose an email.

Send the email, and the recipient will receive it as a text or multimedia message.

Important Notes

Gateways can sometimes filter or block emails that look like spam.
Some carriers may convert multimedia to a link for download rather than embedding the content.
Ensure the recipient's phone plan supports SMS or MMS to receive these messages.
